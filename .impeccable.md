## Design Context

### Users
- Families and local community members
- Ordering dinner from home
- Looking for convenient, quality local food options

### Brand Personality
- "Best and local" — community-focused, trustworthy, warm
- Organic, elegant, close to nature aesthetic
- Approachable yet refined

### Aesthetic Direction
- Organic, elegant, close to nature
- Primary color: Dark green (replacing red)
- Adapts to Telegram theme (light/dark automatically)
- Avoid generic AI aesthetics — distinctive, memorable

### Design Principles
1. **Warm & Natural**: Use colors and spacing that feel organic, not sterile
2. **Community-Focused**: Design that feels local and approachable, not corporate
3. **Elegant Simplicity**: Refined details over decorative elements
4. **Nature-Inspired**: Use the green palette to evoke freshness and quality
5. **Adaptable**: Respect Telegram's theme while maintaining brand identity
